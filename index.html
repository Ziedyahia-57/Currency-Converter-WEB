01.
What makes this extension different?

It combines a suite of highly-requested features including smart format recognition, Convert-on-Select, in-page conversion, customizable formatting, and more.

02.
Can it convert in-page currencies?

Yes, in-page conversion is available in settings. When enabled, the extension automatically detects and converts prices as the page loads. If disabled, you can still convert any value by simply selecting it.

03.
Can I change how prices appear?

You can choose the number format that fits your region or preference, including options for decimal and thousands separators. This ensures converted prices are always easy to read and consistent with your expectations.

04.
Will it slow down my browser?

Not at all. While the extension is around 8 MB due to its feature-rich design, it’s carefully optimized to run efficiently and is continuously improved with each update for smooth performance.

05.
Will I have to pay to use this extension?

No, the extension is completely free, with no sign-ups, subscriptions, or hidden fees. If you find it valuable, you’re welcome to support it with an optional donation, it's entirely up to you and it's greatly appreciated!
